Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SKIFI GROUP LLC and SKIFI CREATIVE HUB LLP ("SkiFi", "we", "us") collect, use and protect your information when you use skifi.co or engage our services.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you provide - name, email address, phone number and project details when you contact us by email, phone, WhatsApp or through the website.
  • Usage information - basic technical data such as browser type, device and pages visited, collected through standard web technologies.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotes;
  • To deliver and manage projects;
  • To improve our website and services;
  • To send occasional updates you have requested.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (such as hosting and analytics providers), within SkiFi Group entities, or where required by law.

4. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above or to meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymise it.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing contact@skifidesigns.com. We will respond within a reasonable period.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. International transfers

As we operate from the United States and India, your information may be processed in either country. We take steps to ensure it is protected consistently with this policy.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact
